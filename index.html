How it works

Care that starts online

Three simple steps. Most concerns are resolved on the first one — you only come in when there’s a real reason to.

  1. 01

    Book your teleconsult

    Pick a time that suits you and book a secure video consult in minutes. No waiting room, no commute.

  2. 02

    Meet your doctor online

    Speak with a licensed clinician by video. Get a diagnosis, prescription, referral, or a clear plan — most concerns end here.

  3. 03

    Come in only if needed

    If you need a physical exam, a procedure, or follow-up, we book your in-clinic visit — with the team already briefed on your case.
